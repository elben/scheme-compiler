### Registers

http://www.eecg.toronto.edu/~amza/www.mindsec.com/files/x86regs.html

```
%eax is the accumulator register.
- ax, ah, al are ways to access this register.
- ax - lower 16 bits
- al - lower 6 bits
- ah - bits 6 - 15

|63..32|31..16|15-8|7-0|
               |AH.|AL.|
               |AX.....|
       |EAX............|
|RAX...................|
```
